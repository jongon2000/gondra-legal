Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jon Gondra Content Hub ("the App") handles data when connecting to TikTok's API.

1. What data is accessed

When authorized via TikTok's OAuth flow, the App may access:

  • Basic profile information (username, display name, avatar)
  • Video content and captions supplied by the account owner for posting
  • Post status information (e.g. success/failure of an upload)

2. How data is used

Data retrieved from TikTok is used solely to display account status and to publish content that I have chosen to upload through the App. Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.

3. Data storage

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to make authorized API calls on my behalf. Tokens are not shared with any third party.

4. Data retention and deletion

I can revoke the App's access at any time via TikTok account settings, which immediately invalidates stored tokens. Content data is retained only as long as needed to complete scheduled posts.

5. Third parties

The App does not share TikTok data with any third-party service.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to gondracrespo@gmail.com.
